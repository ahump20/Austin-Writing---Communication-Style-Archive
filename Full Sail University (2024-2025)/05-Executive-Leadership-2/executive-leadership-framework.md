## PERSONAL BRAND LEADERSHIP FRAMEWORK

**FOUNDATIONAL PREMISE:**  
"Personal branding is a leadership requirement, not a self-promotion campaign." — Glenn Llopis

**BRAND DEVELOPMENT SEQUENCE:**
1. **Identity Excavation** — Uncover authentic leadership strengths and signature traits
2. **Value Articulation** — Define specific leadership value you consistently deliver
3. **Experience Engineering** — Design the total experience others have with your leadership
4. **Consistency Commitment** — Establish disciplined presence across all leadership contexts
5. **Service Orientation** — Focus brand expression on how you serve others, not self-promotion

**LEADERSHIP BRAND METRICS:**
- **Reputation Clarity** — How clearly others can articulate your leadership value
- **Influence Reach** — The expanding circle of your leadership impact
- **Trust Velocity** — Speed at which others place confidence in your leadership
- **Legacy Momentum** — Long-term impact of your leadership contributions
